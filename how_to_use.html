How to Use Chemistry Practice 3.0

A complete guide to getting the most out of your offline chemistry learning platform.

Getting Started

1

Open the App

Double-click index.html to open it in your browser. No internet is required after the file loads.

2

Create an Account

Click Create Account on the login screen. Fill in your username, full name, age, school, and a secure password. Your data is stored only on this device.

3

Install as App (Optional)

In Chrome or Edge, click the install icon in the address bar to install Chemistry Practice as a standalone app on your desktop or phone.

Core Modules

📊 Dashboard

Your home base. See your XP, level, rank, study streak, daily goals, weak topics, latest achievements, and the leaderboard at a glance. A daily chemistry fact and quote greet you each session.

📘 Topic Notes

Comprehensive study notes organised by topic, covering Matter, Atomic Structure, Bonding, Stoichiometry, Acids & Bases, Electrochemistry, Organic Chemistry, and more. Use the search bar to find anything instantly. Bookmark topics to pin them to your dashboard.

🃏 Flashcards

Tap a card to flip it. Use the Got it / Review again buttons to apply spaced repetition — cards you find hard appear more often. Earn XP for every card reviewed.

✏️ Practice Questions

Filter by topic and difficulty (Easy → Master). Get instant feedback with full explanations after each answer. Earn XP for every correct answer; more XP for harder questions.

🧠 Quiz Mode

10 random questions, 60 seconds each. Race the timer and earn bonus XP for speed. Your results feed directly into your Mastery profile.

📝 CBT Exam

Full Computer-Based Test experience: timer, question navigator, mark-for-review, formula sheet, and a built-in calculator. Anti-cheat mode detects tab switches and minimises. After submission, see a full performance report with explanations.

⚛️ Periodic Table

All 118 elements with full data: atomic number, mass, electron configuration, electronegativity, melting/boiling points, discoverer, uses, and fun facts. Click any element to see its full card. Filter by category (metals, non-metals, etc.) or search by name/symbol.

🧮 Chemistry Calculator

25+ calculators: Molar Mass, Molarity, Dilution, Ideal Gas Law, pH/pOH, Half-life, Stoichiometry, Limiting Reagent, Percentage Yield, and many more. Each calculator shows step-by-step working.

⚖️ Equation Balancer

Type an unbalanced equation (e.g. Fe + O2 = Fe2O3) and the engine will balance it automatically, show atom counts before/after, and identify the reaction type.

🧪 Chemistry Dictionary

200+ terms with definitions, examples, and related terms. Search or browse alphabetically.

📐 Formula Handbook

Searchable collection of all major chemistry formulas organised by category. Bookmark favourites.

🔬 Virtual Lab

Mix chemicals, observe reactions, test with indicators, and perform virtual titration. Lab notes record all your experiments.

🤖 AI Tutor

Ask chemistry questions in plain English. The offline tutor uses a keyword database to answer questions about topics, formulas, reactions, and concepts — no internet needed.

🎯 Mastery Tracker

See your accuracy, attempt count, and mastery percentage for every topic. The system identifies your weak areas and recommends what to study next.

📅 Study Planner

Set daily, weekly, and monthly study goals. Use the built-in Pomodoro timer (25 min study / 5 min break). Track your study hours with a visual heatmap.

📈 Analytics

Beautiful charts showing your XP history, quiz accuracy over time, daily activity, strong vs weak topics, and leaderboard rank history.

Admin Panel

Access Admin

On the login screen, click Admin Login and enter the password (default: 123456). The admin panel lets you view all users, reset scores, manage the leaderboard, view system logs, and change the admin password.

Backup & Restore

Export Your Data

Go to Profile → Export Account to download a JSON backup of your account. Use Import Account to restore it on any device.

Tips & Tricks

  • Start with Topic Notes on a subject, then test yourself with Flashcards, then tackle Practice Questions.
  • Use CBT mode for exam simulation — it replicates real exam conditions.
  • Check Mastery weekly to see which topics need more attention.
  • The Equation Balancer can check your manual balancing work.
  • Use the Pomodoro timer in Study Planner for focused study sessions.
  • Earn the Daily Streak badge by logging in every day — even for 5 minutes.
  • Your Admin password can be changed in the admin panel for security.
